About Us

Based in the UAE, Sandscape Architects is a boutique design and landscaping firm dedicated to transforming visions into reality. We specialize in bespoke solutions that blend creativity, functionality and sustainability. Our client-focused approach ensures personalized designs that exceed expectations, drawing inspiration from the UAE's vibrant landscapes.

  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly practices for future generations.
  • Innovation: Creative and forward-thinking solutions.
  • Client-Centricity: Personalized and meaningful designs.
  • Excellence: Quality, precision, and craftsmanship.
  • Integrity: Honesty and ethical practices.

Our Mission

To bring the wonders of nature into homes, offices and galleries, evoking a deep connection to the landscapes that surround us. Each piece we create aims to inspire awe, tranquility and a renewed appreciation for the Earth’s splendor.

Our Vision

To design and deliver innovative, sustainable and personalized landscaping solutions that harmonize with natural environments while reflecting the unique visions of our clients. Guided by creativity, precision and a commitment to eco-friendly practices, we aim to craft inspiring spaces that enhance both the environment and the lives of those who experience them.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What landscaping services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of landscaping services, including design and installation, garden maintenance, hardscaping (patios, walkways, retaining walls), irrigation systems, lighting design, and seasonal cleanups. We also provide customized design consultations tailored to your specific needs.

  • Do you offer landscape design consultations?

    Yes, we offer landscape design consultations where we discuss your ideas, preferences, and budget. During the consultation, we’ll create a design that reflects your style and enhances the beauty of your outdoor space.

  • How long does it take to complete a landscaping project?

    The timeline for completion depends on the complexity of the project. Smaller design or maintenance jobs might take a few days, while larger landscape installations could take several weeks. We’ll provide a timeline estimate during the consultation.

  • What factors influence the cost of my landscaping project?

    The cost of a landscaping project is influenced by several factors, including the size of the area, the complexity of the design, the materials used, and the type of services required. We’ll provide you with a transparent breakdown of costs in our estimate.

  • Can you work with my existing landscape design?

    Absolutely! We can enhance or modify your current landscape design to meet your preferences. Whether it's adding new features, redesigning certain areas, or maintaining what’s already there, we're happy to work with what you have.

  • What types of plants do you recommend for my garden?

    We offer personalized plant recommendations based on your garden's climate, soil, sunlight, and overall aesthetic. We prioritize low-maintenance, drought-resistant plants that are suitable for your area.

  • Do you provide garden maintenance services?

    Yes, we offer ongoing garden maintenance services, including lawn care, pruning, weeding, seasonal cleanups, and pest control. We can create a maintenance plan that fits your schedule and keeps your landscape looking beautiful year-round.

  • How do I prepare my property for a landscaping project?

    Before the project starts, we’ll guide you on any preparations needed, such as clearing debris, marking areas for design, or ensuring access to your property for our team. We’ll work closely with you to make the process as seamless as possible.

  • Do you offer any warranties on your work?

    We stand behind the quality of our work. For installations, we offer warranties on materials and workmanship. The specifics of the warranty will be discussed during the consultation, depending on the project.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and checks. Payment terms and a schedule will be outlined in the contract prior to starting the project.

  • Can you help me with irrigation systems?

    Yes, we design and install efficient irrigation systems that ensure your landscape receives the right amount of water. We also offer maintenance and repairs for existing systems.

  • How do you handle any issues or changes during the project?

    We prioritize clear communication throughout the project. If any issues arise or if changes are requested, we’ll work with you to address them promptly and ensure that the project stays on track and within budget.
